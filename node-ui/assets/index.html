Stats of Politicians

On Twitter

This project was born in September 2022 as a university project. It was improved and re-implemented in March 2023. It aims to analyse politicians' communication strategies on social media as individuals and political groups. Stats are available in different ranges of time. Data are fetched and analysed every day at 00:00 UTC for being published in the next minutes. More details are written on the FAQ page.
